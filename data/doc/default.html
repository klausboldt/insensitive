NMR Tutorial

Insensitive is an application to visualise the NMR experiment on the level of a spin ensemble. The spin system is represented as a semiclassical vector model, as a spin density matrix and as a sum of product operators. Additionally the energy level diagram of the spin system can be displayed.

Read the user's manual for instructions to use Insensitive. The spectrometer hardware is explained here.

Quantum mechanics

To understand NMR and spin physics a basic knowledge of quantum mechanics and the mathematics behind it is necessary.

Fourier Transformation

The pulsed NMR experiments are based on the Fourier transformation, which transforms the free induction decay recorded in the time domain into a spectrum in the frequency domain.

Pulse sequences

A list of pulse sequences for several important NMR experiments with instructions how to simulate them with Insensitive.
