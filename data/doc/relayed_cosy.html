Relayed COSY

The Relayed COrrelation SpectroscopY uses a spin echo sandwich and a third 90° pulse after the COSY sequence to transfer the magnetisation to the next coupling partner and show the second neighbour to a coupled spin pair.

The Relay sequence can be extended to include the third neighbour and more, but is limited by spin relaxation during the sequence. To show all spins in a spin network the TOCSY sequence can be used.
