Gyromagnetic ratio

The gyromagnetic ratio γ (sometimes called the magnetogyric ratio) is the ratio between the angular momentum S of a spin to its magnetic dipole moment μ. It is given in rad s-1 T-1 and is directly proportional to the NMR signal strength. The gyromagnetic ratios for some important nuclei in NMR are given below.
